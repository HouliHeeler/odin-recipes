Cinnamon Toast Crunch Squares

Description

This recipe will lead you down a path from which there is no return. Forget what you thought you knew about snacking, get ahead of the game by stocking up on insulin, and preheat your God-damned oven.

Ingredients

  • Mini Marshmallows
  • Cinnamon Toast Crunch
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Melt butter and mini marshmallows together over medium heat.
  2. Slowly add Cinnamon Toast Crunch and mix gently, careful not to crush the squares into dust.
  3. In a cake pan lined with parchment paper, unload your mixture. Carefully level it out, and move to the fridge for thirty minutes to set.
  4. Remember to turn off the oven that you were so aggressively asked to preheat, despite no apparent call for it.
